Education

D&C 109:7

I started high school at Grayslake Central High School in Grayslake, Illinois. I was a National Honors Society Member there, as well as President of the Music National Honors Society. Halfway through my senior year, my parents moved me and my three younger siblings to Sandy, Utah, where I attended Alta High School for the remainder of my preliminary education.

After I served a two year mission to Stockholm, Sweden, I returned to the United States for higher education. I began as a FlexGE student at BYU, where I got a taste of what higher learning was all about. I served as the FlexGE student council president throughout my time as a FlexGE student.

After three semesters, I officially became a BYU Cougar. I have declared my intention to enter the Marriot School of Business with an emphasis in Marketing.

To prepare for the rigors of the Marketing Program, I've taken many preperatory business courses including

  • MKTG 201 - Marketing Managment
  • FIN 201 - Principles of Finance
  • ACC 200 - Principles of Accounting
  • ECON 110 - Economics Principles + Problems
  • IS 110 - Spreadseets + Business Analysis (A-)
  • IS 201 - Intro to Managment Info Systems
  • COMMS 230 - Intro to Advertizing (A-)
  • MSB 494R - On-Campus Internship (A-)
  • WRTG 150 - Writing Rhetoric (A-)
  • WRTG 312 - Persuasive Writing (A)
  • STDEV 150 - Public Speaking (A-)

Each of these classes has prepared me for further learning and have given me much needed experience.
