About Me

Mosiah 2:41

I was born in Salt Lake City to Garth and Ane Snow. When I was 3 years old, we moved our east to the northern suburbs of Chicago, where I spent the majority of my life.

I loved living in Chicago, but halfway through senior year, my dad told me and my three younger siblings, it was time to go back west. We moved to Sandy, Utah in Salt Lake County, close to lots of family.

I was not very happy with the move. Yes, we were closer to family, but I had to start from scratch at a brand new school with a completely different crowd of people. But I learned a lot about myself through moving. I met and made connections with people who would bless my life in ways I couldn't see at the time. Looking back, I'm very grateful for the challange that moving was, because it pushed me harder to do my best.

I did not have to stay for too long, because a few short months after we moved, I was called to serve a full time mission to Stockholm Sweden. I learned Swedish and taught people about Jesus Christ for 25 months and I could not ask for a better experience.

When I came home, I jumped right into school. Just over a year after returning, I married Emma Huntington, the love of my life, in the Provo City Center Temple. As of June 2023, Emma and I have been happily married for 8 months and we're expecting our first child in January. We currently reside in Provo, Utah.
